About Us

Fraser Valley Bitcoin is a grassroots group that believes bitcoin can be simple, practical and empowering for everyone. We help people understand how to use bitcoin in real life – from securing your own wallet and sending Lightning payments to understanding why Bitcoin’s design matters. We also support local businesses who want to accept bitcoin and are working towards a circular economy where locals earn, spend and save bitcoin right here in the Fraser Valley.

Beginner Resources

Articles & Books

  • Bitcoin.org – Getting Started – overview of wallets, buying & spending bitcoin.
  • Important things to know before using bitcoin – covers wallet security, volatility, irreversibility and privacy.
  • The Bitcoin Standard – a widely read book on the history of money and why Bitcoin matters.
  • My First Bitcoin program – a free 10‑week introduction used by many community groups.

Videos

  • What's the Problem? – short video explaining the problems Bitcoin aims to solve.
  • God Bless Bitcoin – documentary about the moral case for Bitcoin.

Podcasts

  • What Bitcoin Did – interviews with developers, economists and entrepreneurs.
  • Coin Stories with Natalie Brunell – Natalie Brunell is an investing journalist, podcaster and Bitcoin educator who hosts the Coin Stories Podcast.
  • Swan Signal / Swan Lounge – conversations with thought leaders in the space.

We’re continually adding to this list. If you have favourite books, podcasts or videos, let us know at bitcoinchilliwack@gmail.com.
